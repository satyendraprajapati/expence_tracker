Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and login credentials, along with the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions of your transactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide to operate the Service: authenticating your account, storing and displaying your expenses, and generating spending summaries and category breakdowns. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely in our database and are associated only with your account. We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data, subject to any retention required by law.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your expense data with third-party services or advertisers. Should we ever integrate with a third-party provider (for example, for email delivery or hosting), we will only share the minimum data necessary for that service to function, and any such provider will be bound to protect your data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach out to us through the contact details provided on your account page.
